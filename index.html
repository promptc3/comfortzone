Living with ADHD

Neurodivergence in a developing nation with bare minimum awareness of mental health issues has certainly taken a toll on me. My life upto now has been extremely difficult. From family and friends to studies, everything has been an uphill battle, but things have been a lot better lately. I was finally able to get medication after switching around multiple pschyiatrists. I have learned a lot about mental health issues like depression, anxiety, ADHD, trauma and developed multiple coping mechanisms in order to survive. Multiple comorbidities and multiple interests, it is that kind of disorder. Although if you live in India do reach out to me for any help related to ADHD.
